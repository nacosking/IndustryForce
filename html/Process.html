Step by Step The Recruitment Process
5 Stages — From Brief to Placement
Step 01
Requirement Discussion

We start with a detailed conversation about your factory's needs — roles, skill levels, volumes, timelines, and specific technical requirements. Nothing is assumed.

Step 02
Technical Screening

Candidates undergo technical interviews conducted by our team. We assess trade knowledge, machine experience, and practical competence — not just what's on a CV.

Step 03
Skill Grading

Each candidate receives a skill grade (1–5) based on experience, trade test results, and interview performance. You see the grade before the CV — so you know what you're getting.

Step 04
Shortlist & Presentation

You receive a shortlist of verified, graded candidates with full profiles. Only workers who meet your exact specification are presented — no padding, no guesswork.

Step 05
Visa & Immigration Coordination

For international candidates, we coordinate with our UK immigration partner. The employer handles sponsorship — we support and manage the process end to end.
