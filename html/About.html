Our Story

From the Shopfloor
to Your Shortlist

Our founder spent 15 years as a machinist — operating CNC lathes, milling machines, and precision grinding equipment across multiple manufacturing facilities.

That hands-on background means we understand the difference between a competent operator and an exceptional one. We know what tolerances mean in practice, how a well-run production line operates, and exactly what skills your factory needs.

Over the following 25 years, this manufacturing knowledge was channelled into technical recruitment — building an international network of trade-tested professionals and a screening process that factories trust.
