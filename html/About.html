Our Story

From the Shopfloor
to Your Shortlist

Our founder spent 15+ years working as a manifacutring hand on experience — exprience in all kind of tool and machinery equipment. That time on the floor wasn't just a career; it was where deep relationships were built with the skilled tradespeople who keep production lines running.

Those years also made one thing clear: the difference between a good hire and a great one is something a CV rarely shows. It takes someone who's stood at the machine, hit the tolerances, and managed the pressure of a live production schedule to know what to look for.

Today, that manufacturing background and the network built over decades power everything we do — connecting overseas factories with skilled workers who are genuinely ready to perform from day one.
